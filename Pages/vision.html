Our Vision

As the world shifts towards Mobility as a Service (MaaS), the demand for efficient, environmentally responsible fleet cleaning solutions grows. The traditional car wash model, with its high water usage and environmental impact, is no longer viable in this new era.

SpeedBuddy envisions leading the car wash industry as the number one provider of sustainable, waterless car washing care.

We aim to solve the environmental challenges of traditional car washes and inspire businesses to adopt eco-friendly practices that protect our planet and enhance efficiency.

The Path Forward

We are more than just a cleaning service. We are pioneers in the industry, dedicated to research and development in mobility and AI integration. Our vision extends beyond today’s challenges, as we forge a path toward a cleaner, more sustainable future.
